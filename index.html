What is Salvus?

Salvus is a free-to-use program where students can submit a 10-second form in the event that they have to evacuate from their school. Both parents and school administrators are instantly contacted with students' safety status and location.

Administrators can create a custom database to store student information including parent contact information and student statuses to ease contact between parents, administrators, and authorities.
